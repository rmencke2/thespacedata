Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create brand assets?
Open the app and select your asset type. Choose a template, customize your content, preview it, and publish - all in under 60 seconds!
Can I preview my assets before publishing?
Yes! Use the preview feature to see exactly how your assets will look across different contexts and formats before you publish them.
What's included in Premium?
Premium includes access to all templates, advanced customization options, priority support, and removes any usage limitations. Start with a free trial!
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Influzer → Cancel Subscription.
Is my data private?
Yes! We take your privacy seriously. All your data is stored securely and we don't share it with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.
I'm having trouble signing in
Make sure you have an active internet connection. For Apple Sign In, ensure "Sign In with Apple" is enabled in your Apple ID settings. For Google Sign In, make sure you're signed into your Google account on your device.
How do I export my assets?
After creating and previewing your asset, tap the publish button. You can save to your photo library or share directly to your social media platforms.

Subscription Support

For subscription-related questions or issues:
