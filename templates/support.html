Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the Color Picker?

The Color Picker allows you to explore and select colors for your projects. Simply browse through the color palette, select colors, and generate palettes that work well together.

Can I download the logos I generate?

Yes! Once you generate a logo using our Logo Generator, you can download it in various formats. The download options will be available after generation.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Please review our Privacy Policy for detailed information about how we protect your data.

Are the services free?

Many of our tools are available for free use. Some advanced features may require a subscription. Check individual tool pages for specific pricing information.

How do I report a bug?

If you encounter a bug or technical issue, please email us at support@thespacedata.com with a description of the issue and steps to reproduce it.

Account Issues

Having trouble with your account? Common issues include:

  • Forgotten password - Use the password reset link on the login page
  • Account locked - Contact support for assistance
  • Email verification - Check your spam folder for verification emails

Feature Requests

Have an idea for a new feature or improvement? We'd love to hear from you! Send your suggestions to support@thespacedata.com.
